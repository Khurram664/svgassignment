1) Enter command in the text area to generate circles, reactangles and polygon
2) Enter commad c 20 40 15  that will generate circle with its centre at coordinate 20,40 with a radius of 15
3) Enter commad r 20 40 15 18 that will generate circle with its starting point at coordinate 20,40 with width 15 and height 18.
4) Like wise a command of p 200,10 250,190 160,210 wil generate polygon with many sides on  given coordinates.
5) Click draw to execute commands and see results in SVG panel.
6) Click clear to remove all svg elements and error.
